## 📧 What is Phishing?
Phishing is a type of cyber attack where criminals try to trick you into revealing sensitive information, such as passwords or credit card numbers.  
They often use emails, fake websites, or messages that look real.  

---

## 🕵️‍♂️ How to Recognize Phishing
- Check the sender’s email carefully  
- Look for spelling mistakes or unusual formatting  
- Hover over links to see the real URL before clicking  
- Be wary of urgent messages asking for money or credentials  

---

## 🔒 Protect Yourself
- Never click on suspicious links  
- Enable 2FA wherever possible  
- Use browser extensions that warn about unsafe websites  
- Verify requests with official contacts  

---

## 📌 Common Phishing Examples
- Fake bank alerts  
- Social media messages claiming "You’ve won a prize!"  
- Emails claiming account suspension or urgent verification  

---

Phishing attacks can happen to anyone, but with awareness, you can prevent most attacks from succeeding.
