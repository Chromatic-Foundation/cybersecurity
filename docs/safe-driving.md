## 🚦 Safe Driving Principles

Safe driving is not just obeying laws — it’s about **anticipating hazards, controlling speed, and being aware of your surroundings.**

---

## 🧠 Speed Management

Driving at or below speed limits gives you more reaction time to avoid risks:  

- In urban areas, you never know when pedestrians will cross.  
- On highways, vehicles around you may change lanes suddenly.  
- Reducing speed by even a few km/h can significantly lower the chance of a crash.  

Excessive speed is one of the most common causes of serious accidents in Romania — leading to high fatality rates compared with other EU countries.:contentReference[oaicite:19]{index=19}

## 👀 Defensive Driving Techniques  

### 🔹 Always scan ahead  
Look at least 3–4 seconds ahead to spot hazards early.

### 🔹 Maintain safe following distance  
Keep enough space between you and the vehicle in front — especially in rain or on highways.

### 🔹 Check mirrors frequently  
Expect sudden moves from other drivers, especially at intersections.

---

## 🚗 Driving in Different Conditions

### 🌧️ Rain
Wet roads reduce grip — reduce your speed; brake earlier.

### 🌙 Night
Visibility is lower — use dipped headlights, and don’t overdrive your headlights.

### ❄️ Winter
Snow and ice demand slower speeds and smooth steering.

---

## 🧩 Modern Safety Technologies

Using safety features like **ABS**, **ESC**, and **lane assist** can help prevent loss of control. These systems don’t replace skill — but they help.
