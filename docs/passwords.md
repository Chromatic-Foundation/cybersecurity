## 🔐 Why Strong Passwords Matter
Passwords are the first line of defense against hackers. Using weak or repeated passwords can put your personal data, emails, and financial information at risk.  

A strong password should be:
- At least 12 characters long  
- Include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special symbols  
- Unique for every account  

---

## 🛠 Tools to Protect Your Accounts
- **Password Managers**: Keep all your passwords secure in one place (e.g., LastPass, Bitwarden)  
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**: Adds an extra verification step, like a code sent to your phone  
- **Recovery Options**: Always update backup email and phone numbers  

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes
- Using the same password across multiple websites  
- Choosing obvious passwords like "123456" or "password"  
- Ignoring alerts about data breaches  

---

## ✅ Tips for Everyday Security
- Change your passwords regularly  
- Enable 2FA on all accounts that support it  
- Avoid sharing passwords with friends or over chat apps  

This guide helps you stay secure at home, protecting your digital life from hackers.
